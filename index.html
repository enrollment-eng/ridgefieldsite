At Ridgefield Collegiate Engineering Prep, we combine rigorous one-on-one STEM instruction with hands-on engineering projects and career mentorship so students graduate with both the skills and the portfolio colleges notice.

Personalized Tutoring

Targeted 1:1 sessions in math, physics, and programming — aligned to the student’s academic needs and College Preparation Analysis (CPA).

Project-Based Learning

Monthly mentor sessions with engineers and college-level mentors to build résumés, leadership stories, and realistic college roadmaps.

Our Tutors & Mentors
